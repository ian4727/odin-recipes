Shrimp paste Omelette

Not your usual beaten eggs!

Tired of the old, boring scrambled eggs? Try this recipe if you fancy a different twist to your breakfast staple!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • Shrimp paste
  • Red chili

Steps:

  1. Heat a pan, then add oil
  2. Saute onion, garlic, red chilis and tomatoes
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl and then add to the pan
  4. Toss the pan lightly and turn off heat
  5. Best serve with rice
