Vegetable noodles

Vegetarian noodle to satisfy your chinese food craving.

Without any meat but is not any less appetizing. This noodle recipe is easy to make, cheap and more importantly, super healthy.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh noodles
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • Bell pepper
  • Broccoli
  • Oyster sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles in a pot of water.
  2. Saute minced onion and garlic.
  3. Add the vegetables.
  4. Once vegetables are kinda soft, add oyster sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
