Banana Cake

The perfect dessert after a hearty meal

You know that feeling when you're full after lunch but still want to snack something sweet? No? Okay, no excuses then, I still fancy another plate of carbs after finishing two.

Ingredients:

  • Bananas
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix flour and baking powder
  3. In a separate bowl, mix mashed bananas, sugar and melted butter
  4. Add eggs
  5. Pour the banana mixture to the first bowl
  6. Lightly mix and fold but do not overdo it
  7. Let cook in the oven until it gets brown
